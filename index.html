FAQ

Common Questions

Straight answers to the things people always ask before booking a demo.

Is the demo really free?

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no trial account, no strings. It's a 30-minute conversation and live demo — whether you go further is entirely up to you.

How long does an Odoo implementation take?

For most SMEs we go live within 6–12 weeks. Simple setups can be faster. Complex multi-company or custom integrations take longer. We give you an honest timeline in the first session.

Can we migrate data from our current system?

Yes. We handle migrations from QuickBooks, Xero, SAP, Excel, and most legacy ERPs. We've done this enough times that it rarely surprises us.

What if Odoo doesn't fit our workflow?

We customise Odoo to fit your processes — not the other way around. If after the demo we genuinely think Odoo isn't right for your stage or industry, we'll tell you that instead of wasting your time.

What does Odoo cost?

Odoo's community edition is open-source and free. The Enterprise edition (more features, support) starts from ~$24 USD/user/month. Implementation and setup is a separate project fee — we'll scope that clearly in the demo.

Do you provide ongoing support after go-live?

Yes. We offer monthly support retainers covering system updates, user training, bug fixes, and feature additions. Most clients stay with us for years after their initial go-live.

Is Odoo cloud or on-premise?

Both. Odoo.sh (cloud) is the most popular — fully managed, auto-updated, accessible anywhere. We also deploy on-premise if your company has data residency requirements.

We're a small team. Is Odoo right for us?

Odoo scales well from 5-person startups to 500-person enterprises. We've implemented it for both. If you're outgrowing Excel or QuickBooks, the demo will show you exactly what the upgrade looks like.
