Got Questions?

The Ones We Hear Most

How long does implementation actually take?
Most implementations go live in 4–8 weeks. The timeline depends on the number of modules, your existing data, and how quickly your team can participate in review sessions. We give you a firm timeline after the discovery call — and we stick to it.
Do I need to know anything about Odoo before starting?
Absolutely not. We handle the entire technical side — configuration, data migration, testing, and deployment. We just need you to know your business. Staff training is included in every engagement so your team is confident from day one.
What if my team doesn't adopt the new system?
Adoption is our #1 focus. We involve key team members from day one, build the system around their actual workflows, and provide hands-on training. We don't disappear post-launch — we stay available for the first 30 days to ensure smooth adoption.
How much does it cost?
Every project is scoped individually — a 5-person service business has very different needs from a 50-person manufacturer. We provide a detailed proposal after the free discovery call. Most mid-sized implementations start from USD 5,000–8,000. ROI is typically achieved within 3–6 months.
Can you integrate Odoo with our existing tools?
Yes. We regularly integrate Odoo with Shopify, WooCommerce, Lazada, Shopee, WhatsApp, Slack, Xero, QuickBooks, and more. If there's an API, we can connect it. Custom integrations are scoped during the solution design phase.
Do you provide support after go-live?
Yes — every project includes a 30-day post-launch support period. After that, we offer flexible retainer packages for ongoing support, updates, and new feature development. You'll always have a named consultant you can reach directly, not a ticket system.
