Our Story

Built for Businesses That Are Ready to Grow

TechNext was founded in 2018 by a team of enterprise software veterans who saw a gap in the market — growing businesses in Southeast Asia deserved the same powerful tools as large corporations, without the complexity or the price tag.

We chose Odoo as our platform of choice because it uniquely combines deep functionality with flexibility. Today, we are a certified Odoo Ready Partner with implementations spanning manufacturing, retail, F&B, logistics, and professional services.

Headquartered in Singapore with on-the-ground teams in Vietnam and the Philippines

Deep expertise across Odoo Accounting, Inventory, Manufacturing, HR, and AI automation modules

Average go-live time of 8–12 weeks with dedicated post-implementation support
