Frequently Asked Questions

What happens during the free consultation?
We'll spend 30 minutes understanding your current business processes, pain points, and goals. Our consultant will then recommend which Odoo modules are right for you and provide a rough implementation timeline and cost estimate — at no charge.
How long does an Odoo implementation take?
Most of our implementations take 8–12 weeks from kickoff to go-live. Larger enterprise projects with heavy customisation may take 3–6 months. We'll give you a precise timeline during the consultation.
Do you support businesses outside Singapore?
Yes. We have dedicated teams in Vietnam and the Philippines, and we work remotely with clients across Southeast Asia including Malaysia, Indonesia, and Thailand. All consultations are conducted online via Google Meet or Zoom.
What is the cost of Odoo implementation?
Implementation costs vary depending on the number of modules, level of customisation, data migration complexity, and training requirements. Entry-level implementations start from SGD 8,000. We'll provide a detailed quote after the free consultation.
Do you offer post-implementation support?
Absolutely. We offer flexible support packages including dedicated account managers, SLA-based helpdesk, system updates, and ongoing training. Many of our clients have been with us for 3+ years after their initial go-live.
